If you're reading this on an older build: click the `v0.x.y` version number in the app (bottom-left corner on recent builds, top-right on older ones) and follow the download prompt — on Windows / Linux AppImage / signed macOS it installs itself; otherwise it drops the installer in your Downloads. Or download directly: https://github.com/dmarzzz/shape-rotator-os/releases/latest
